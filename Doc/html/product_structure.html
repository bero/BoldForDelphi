Product Structure

Hierarchical Bill of Materials modeling

Overview

Product Structure demonstrates hierarchical data modeling in Bold. Create Bill of Materials (BOM) structures with products containing subassemblies and parts.

Features

  • Recursive structures - Products containing products
  • Quantity tracking - How many of each part needed
  • Cost rollup - Calculate total cost from components
  • Tree visualization - Display product hierarchy
